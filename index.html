Welcome to Hi-Tech World School

FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S OFFICE

It is a blessing to lead the Team at Hi-Tech World School. Our goal is to pursue academic excellence by motivationg and empowering students to be lifelong learners and productive contributors of an ever-changing close-knit Society.
We offer a challenging educational program, supported with exceptional performing arts and an ever-expanding Sports program alongside a host of opportunities for every child to maximize their god given potential. Our staff is devoted to nurture the students for life, grooming them to face the challenges of tomorrow and encourage them to be socially relevant. With our finest faculty we mentor constantly to develop new skills.
Our investment in the school infrastructure demonstrates our commitment to develop exceptional Learning Spaces. Our students work with modern technologies with classrooms digitally backed with contemporary learning tools. It is our vision to develop students who demonstrate resilience, respect , integrity and independence.

Inspirational Thoughts

न चोरहार्यं न च राजहार्यं न भ्रातृभाज्यं न च भारकारी ।
व्यये कृते वर्धते एव नित्यं विद्याधनं सर्वधन प्रधानम् ॥
Translation..

Education is the best wealth among all. No one can steal it, no state can snatch it.
It cannot be divided among the brothers and it's not heavy to carry. As one consumes or spend, it increases; as one shares, it expands.

अलसस्य कुतो विद्या अविद्यस्य कुतो धनम् ।
अधनस्य कुतो मित्रममित्रस्य कुतः सुखम् ॥
Translation..

The one who is lazy and doesn't put in efforts is unable to aquire knowledge.
Wealth does not come to the one without knowledge and qualification. In such bad times, one connot make good friends and happiness is far way from him.

विद्यां ददाति विनयं विनयाद् याति पात्रताम् ।
पात्रत्वात् धनमाप्नोति धनात् धर्मं ततः सुखम् ॥
Translation..

Knowledge brings humility; from humility comes worthiness;
with worthiness one attains wealth; with wealth one is able to perform his
duties in a better way; and in performing his duties one attains happiness.

नाभिषेको न संस्कारः सिंहस्य क्रियते वने ।
विक्रमार्जितसत्त्वस्य स्वयमेव मृगेंद्रता ॥
Translation..

There is no official coronation ceremony held to declare lion as the king of jungle. He becomes the king by his own attributes and heroic actions.Similarly, a person can achieve a position above other with his virtues and efforts.
